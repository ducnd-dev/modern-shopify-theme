# 💼 Business Setup Guide for Shopify Theme Store

Complete business infrastructure setup for successful theme store operations.

## 🏢 Business Foundation

### 1. Legal Business Structure
```
Recommended Business Types:
├── LLC (Limited Liability Company)
│   ├── Personal asset protection
│   ├── Tax flexibility
│   ├── Simple formation process
│   └── Professional credibility
├── Corporation (C-Corp or S-Corp)
│   ├── Maximum liability protection
│   ├── Easier to raise capital
│   ├── More complex tax structure
│   └── Greater regulatory requirements
└── Sole Proprietorship
    ├── Simplest structure
    ├── Direct tax implications
    ├── Personal liability exposure
    └── Suitable for solo developers
```

### 2. Business Registration
```bash
Required Steps:
1. Choose business name
2. Register with state/province
3. Obtain EIN (US) or Business Number (Canada)
4. Open business bank account
5. Get business insurance
6. Register for taxes
7. Obtain necessary licenses
```

### 3. Financial Setup
```
Banking Requirements:
- Business checking account
- Business savings account
- Business credit card
- Payment processing account
- Accounting software subscription

Tax Considerations:
- Quarterly tax payments
- Business expense tracking
- Professional tax advisor
- International tax compliance (if applicable)
```
